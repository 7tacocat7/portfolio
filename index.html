About me

My name is Carson Canales. I was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas.I was raised in a modest household, with one brother and two loving parents. My mother was a teacher and my father a judge. My heritage is hispanic. English is my first language, however i also speak spanish. In my junior year of highschool i was fortunate enough to live on the island of Tenerife, which is both the biggest island in the Canary islands and the tallest mountain in Spain. The time spent overseas has given me cultural diversity and life experience that i value greatly. Due to this experience, as you might expect, I have become an avid traveler and adventurer. In my most recent travels i voyaged to 12 different countries accross Europe over the span of 4 months. Traveling, however, is not my only passion! I have many hobbies and passions. Here are a few; technology, robotics, software development, hardware development, manufacturing, industrial design, skateboarding, animation, cinima, running!!! "
